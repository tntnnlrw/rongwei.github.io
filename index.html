Short Bio

I am currently a forth-year Ph.D's student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Zhi Wang. My research interests are to accelerating machine learning training from communication and computation.

I was a research intern in the System and Networking Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia. I have published several papers in IEEE INFOCOM, Transactions on Mobile Computing, and served as a reviewer for ICML, NeurIPS, MM, and other conferences.
